Lasagna


Description

classic beef lasagna, vegetable lasagna, spinach and ricotta lasagna, chicken Alfredo lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can 28 oz crushed tomatoes

Steps

  1. Cook the beef and onions
  2. Add tomatoes and seasonings
  3. Medium bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, egg until smooth
  4. while sauce is simmering cook the lasagna noodles to package directions
  5. Preheat oven to 375 F
